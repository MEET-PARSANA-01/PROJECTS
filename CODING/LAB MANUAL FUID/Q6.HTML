PANEER BHURJI

Paneer Bhurji is a popular Indian vegetarian dish made from crumbled paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cooked with a variety of spices, vegetables, and herbs. It's a flavorful and protein-packed dish often enjoyed with roti, paratha, or rice. Whether for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, Paneer Bhurji is a comforting and delicious choice for any meal!

INGREDIENTS

  • 200g Paneer (Cottage Cheese), crumbled
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Tomato, finely chopped
  • 1 Green Chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Coriander Leaves, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
